Liver Diseases Analysis

About the Project

This project leverages YOLOv11, a cutting-edge object detection and segmentation model, to identify and analyze liver diseases from medical images. It detects four distinct classes, aiding in early diagnosis and treatment planning.

Liver Abscess

A collection of pus in the liver, often caused by bacterial infection.

Liver Cyst

Fluid-filled sacs within the liver, typically benign but may require monitoring.

Liver Tumor

Abnormal growths in the liver, which can be benign or malignant (cancerous).

No Detection

Indicates a healthy liver with no visible abnormalities.
